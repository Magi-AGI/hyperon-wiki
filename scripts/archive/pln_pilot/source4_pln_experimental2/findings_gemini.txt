## §1 — nuPLN Theory (Global Universal Distribution)
- **C-4-1: The Solomonoff Pivot:** `nuPLN` (documented in `docs/nuPLN/nuPLN.tex`) is a theoretical reformulation of PLN that embraces a global probability distribution $\nu$, mirroring Solomonoff Universal Induction. [docs/nuPLN/nuPLN.tex:42]
- **C-4-2: Observable Predicates:** Observations in nuPLN are outcomes of evaluated predicates (probabilistic programs), rather than bit strings. [docs/nuPLN/nuPLN.tex:46]
- **C-4-3: Dissolution of Intensional/Extensional Split:** The manuscript proposes dropping the concept side (Inheritance) entirely in favor of the predicate side (Implication), using the indicator-function/satisfying-set isomorphism. [docs/nuPLN/nuPLN.tex:178]
- **C-4-4: Manuscript Status:** Confirmed as an incomplete draft. Sections 4.1 and 4.2 (Deriving Rules) end with "NEXT" placeholders. [docs/nuPLN/nuPLN.tex:297, 318]

## §2 — Extended Truth-Value Model
- **C-4-5: Bl and Pr Verified:** Contrary to earlier hypotheses, `Bl` (Boolean) and `Pr` (Probability) are **degenerate STVs**, not Indefinite or Distributional TVs.
  - They return a hardcoded `max-count` of 1e9. [experimental/early-pln/common/truthvalue/TruthValue.metta:44]
  - They return a confidence of 1.0. [experimental/early-pln/common/truthvalue/TruthValue.metta:93]
- **C-4-6: STV as Posterior:** The implementation treats `STV` as the mode of a Beta distribution, using Jeffreys prior ($\alpha=\beta=0.5$). [experimental/early-pln/common/truthvalue/TruthValue.metta:34]

## §3 — Implemented Rules (Source Delta)
- **C-4-7: Implication Direct Introduction (IDI):** A sophisticated inductive rule not found in production PLN repositories.
  - Implemented as `IDIBase` and `IDIRecursive`. [experimental/early-pln/pln/dependent-types/ImplicationDirectIntroductionDTL.metta:101]
  - **Evidence Order:** Uses sorted IDs (`h < a`) to ensure evidence is never double-counted. The author realization: "far more efficient to enforce the order... than to prove non-membership." [experimental/early-pln/pln/dependent-types/ImplicationDirectIntroductionDTL.metta:148]
- **C-4-8: Rule Scarcity:** This repository is a research sandbox and lacks 90% of the book's rules (no quantifiers, temporal, or concept-geometry).

## §4 — Maxima Verification (Conjunction Introduction)
- **C-4-9: Structural Evidence:** Experiments in `experimental/truth-value-formula/conjunction-introduction/` conclude that the **structure** of a composite predicate itself provides evidence. [experimental/truth-value-formula/conjunction-introduction/README.md:255]
- **C-4-10: Independence Convergence:** Verified using Stirling approximation that uniformly sampled underlying sets converge to a Dirac delta at $pA \cdot pB$ as universe size $n \to \infty$, justifying the independence assumption in large systems. [experimental/truth-value-formula/conjunction-introduction/README.md:120]
