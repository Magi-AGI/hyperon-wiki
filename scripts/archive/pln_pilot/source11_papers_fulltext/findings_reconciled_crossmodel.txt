## §2 — Differentiated insights (one or two models only)

### D1. Codex: idempotency obstacle in Finset (Multiset variant) — material for cluster narrative
Codex C-B3-3 / Claude C-B3-3: world-model-calculus.tex L191-195 acknowledges that Finset union is idempotent (`W ∪ W = W`), which breaks `evidence_add` for non-disjoint pairs. Multiset sum is the free commutative monoid. **This is methodologically critical** — the WM calculus only works for disjoint stores under Finset; Multiset is required for general use. Wiki should note this when describing PathMap evidence. Gemini did not extract this.
