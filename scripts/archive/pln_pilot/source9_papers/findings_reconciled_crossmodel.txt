### 2.2 [X3] Specifications are THAT, not HOW

The papers specify atom types, sum-product equations, error bounds, and ontological arguments. They DO NOT specify:

**Wiki implication**: cards should NOT present the FactorGraph papers and the Chaining-on-MORK paper as a single integrated proposal. They are two parallel architectural visions.
