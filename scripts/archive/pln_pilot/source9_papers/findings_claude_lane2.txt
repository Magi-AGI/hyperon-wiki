**Core claims:**
- C-1: Confidence updating for the PLN AND rule should use a "weakness-composition" formulation in an information-theoretic quantale Q_E = ([0,∞) × [0,1], ≤, ⊗, I), where weakness w = 1 - c = k/(e + k).
- C-2: Weakness composition under independence is probabilistic OR: w₁ ⊕ w₂ = 1 - (1-w₁)(1-w₂). Combined w_{A∧B} = 1 - (1-w_A)(1-w_B)(1-w_struct).
- C-3: Strength under independence is multiplicative: s_{A∧B} = s_A · s_B; with correlation ρ, an additive correction √(s_A(1-s_A) s_B(1-s_B)) appears.
- C-4: Structural weakness has an information-theoretic identity: w_struct(A,B) = H(A∧B | A∨B)/H(A∧B) = 1 - I(A;B)/min(H(A),H(B)).
- C-5: Evidence overlap factor between two evidence sets uses an exponential decay over normalized mutual information: overlap = exp(-λ I(e_A; e_B)/H_min). Independent evidence ⇒ overlap=1; full dependence ⇒ exp(-λ).
- C-6: Final confidence form: c_{A∧B} = c_A · c_B · overlap / (1 + k · w_combined).
- C-7: Estimation procedure for empirical use: Miller-Madow corrected entropies for discrete data, KDE or k-NN (Kraskov-style ψ-digamma) for continuous.
- C-8: PLN/NARS unification claim: NARS conjunction f×c formula c = c₁c₂(f_A + f_B - f_A f_B) is recovered from the PLN-with-overlap formula in the high-confidence limit (k→0) with overlap = inclusion-exclusion (f_A + f_B - f_A f_B).

**Intended implementation target:** PLN AND/conjunction TruthValue formulas for the production PLN3/PLN/ confidence model.

**Current implementation status:** PAPER-ONLY / ASPIRATIONAL.

**Focus Question #1: Does `PLN AND Confidence Framework` explain the pln0.9 multiplicative-confidence pivot?**

Answer: **NO.** The two are different mathematical lineages.

Evidence:
- `PLN AND Confidence Framework.md` proposes weakness-composition w_{A∧B} = 1 - (1-w_A)(1-w_B)(1-w_struct) plus an *information-theoretic* overlap factor exp(-λ I(e_A; e_B)/H_min).
- pln0.9 (Source 3, Peter Isaev `7ffce05` 2026-03-12) introduces multiplicative confidence with strength-entanglement of the form c_AND ≈ c_1·c_2·s_1/(s_1 + s_2 - s_1·s_2) (or similar — Source 3 had the canonical formulas) plus a `Truth_or` heuristic.
- The two CONVERGE conceptually on "confidence should multiply, with a strength-aware modulation" but they DIVERGE on the modulation. The framework paper uses information-theoretic mutual-information overlap; pln0.9 uses inclusion-exclusion-style strength terms.
- The PLN AND Confidence Framework paper does claim to RECOVER NARS-style formulas under specific limits (k→0, uniform priors), and the pln0.9 production formulas are arguably descended from NARS-aware lineage. So they are RELATED via NARS but NOT directly derivable from each other's exact equations.

Verdict: **Partial / NO**. Theoretical underpinning of pln0.9's general direction (multiplicative confidence with strength entanglement) is *consonant with* this paper's spirit, but the SPECIFIC FORMULAS in pln0.9 do not appear in this paper, and the paper's core mathematical machinery (information quantale, Miller-Madow entropy, exponential MI-decay overlap) does not appear in pln0.9 code.
