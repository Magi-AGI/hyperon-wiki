## Book-to-code verification targets

From the Source 1 cross-model reconciliation, these items have specific expected code-level manifestations in PLN3. Please verify.

### V1 — Symmetric Modus Ponens formula
Source 1 resolved in favor of the **multiplication form** `sB = sA·simAB + c·(1−sA)·(1+simAB)` (not division). Is PLN3's `symmetricModusPonens` consistent with this? Transcribe the formula as implemented.

### V2 — Default term probability `c`
The 2008 book uses `c = 0.02` as the default-term-probability constant for heuristic Modus Ponens. Does PLN3 use this value? If configurable, where?

### V3 — Inference trails
The book introduces bounded-length inference trails (~10–20 atoms) to prevent circular inference and TV inflation. Does PLN3's `Deriver.metta` maintain trails ("stamps")? What bound?

### V4 — Revision with count drift
The book warns that revision with `c > 0` drifts strengths toward 1. Does PLN3's revision rule use a nonzero overlap constant?

### V5 — Rule list completeness
CLAUDE.md claims rules: Revision, Modus Ponens, Symmetric Modus Ponens, Deduction, Induction, Abduction, Evaluation Implication, Inversion, Negation Elimination, Equivalence to Implication, Transitive Similarity. Verify each is actually present and has a formula; note any absent or stub.

### V6 — Missing from PLN3 relative to book
The book has >60 rules. Definite misses to call out if absent: concept-geometry deduction, distributional TV / matrix deduction, intensional-inheritance mixed cases, temporal/causal rules (PredictiveImplication, SeqAND etc.), quantifier rules, multideduction.
