## 1. Highest-signal cross-model deltas (book vs. PLN3)

### 1.1 [X3] PLN3 is a compact STV-only subset, NOT a broad port of the 2008 book
All three models independently: truth-value machinery is uniformly `(stv strength confidence)` â€” no IndefiniteTruthValue, no DistributionalTruthValue, no count form, no second-order TVs. The 2008 book's three-tier TV hierarchy (Simple â†’ Indefinite â†’ Distributional) is collapsed to one level.

### 1.2 [X3] Inference control is forward-chaining beam search, NOT BIT / BC / MAB

No backward chaining, no BIT expansion, no multi-armed bandit scoring, no interestingness metric, no Rule of Choice fallback hierarchy. The book's elaborate control theory is replaced by 3 config knobs: `MaxSteps=20, TaskQueueSize=20, BeliefQueueSize=200`.
