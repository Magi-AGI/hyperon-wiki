## Goal

Produce a **provenance and abandonment analysis** of URE relative to the MeTTa PLN line. Three priority questions:

1. **What's in URE that the MeTTa line abandoned** — quantifier rules, temporal/causal hooks, distributional TVs, FactorGraph adjacency, intensional/extensional conversions, multideduction, BRGI, OBI, attention/importance — is any of this actually here in C++?

3. **Bit-rot diagnostic** — what specifically broke / was disabled / left behind. Multiple commits explicitly disable tests ("Disable backward chainer unit tests. They have bit-rotted.")
