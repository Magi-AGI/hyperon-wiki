V7-1 [AUTH-ON-READ-MIRROR]                      LOAD-BEARING PRE-IMPLEMENTATION GATE
  Source: Card D 17120 §6 + Q15 reviewer consensus.
  Statement: PATCH-5 read tools MUST NOT bypass Decko read-rule
  enforcement. The AtomSpace mirror contains atoms for every card the
  integrate hook fired on, including private/restricted cards
  (read_rule_class / read_rule_id on cards.* table). Without enforcement,
  PATCH-5 = read-rule bypass = security regression on the existing Decko
  deployment.
  Resolution options (recorded for PATCH-5 design pass):
    (a) Encode read_rule into the atom; query layer enforces — long-run
        answer; requires auth-aware encoding (Phase 5+ deferred per
        Source 5 §10).
    (b) Per-agent partitioned mirrors — incompatible with single-process
        Hyperon Space at HEAD.
    (c) Public-only Phase 4 mirror — filter restricted cards out of the
        integrate hook before they reach the atom Space.
    (d) No agent reads in Phase 4 — Phase 4 mirror for trusted internal
        tooling only; agent-readable mirror deferred to Phase 5+.
  Phase 4 default: (c) or (d). Both let Phase 4 ship without an
  authorization regression. (a) is the long-run answer but blocked on
  auth-aware encoding deferral. Choice goes to PATCH-5 implementation
  design conversation.
  Status: ADOPT. Block PATCH-5 exposure to non-admin agents until
  enforcement design is chosen.
