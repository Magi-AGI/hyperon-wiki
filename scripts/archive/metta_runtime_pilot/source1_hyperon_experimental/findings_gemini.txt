### §2. Per-Question Extraction (S1.Q1 - S1.Q12)

**S1.Q1: MeTTa Interpreter Semantics**
- **Verdict: [SMALL-STEP-INTERPRETER]**
- **Evidence:** `lib/src/metta/interpreter.rs` defines `pub fn interpret_step(mut state: InterpreterState) -> InterpreterState` (L269) and `fn interpret_stack(...)` (L374). It is a stack-based reduction engine.
- **Non-determinism:** `SuperposeOp` in `stdlib/core.rs` (L353) and `interpret_step` handle multiple results by spawning branches in the interpreter state.

**S1.Q2: MeTTa Type System**
- **Verdict: [DYNAMIC-CHECKED]**
- **Evidence:** `lib/src/metta/types.rs` implements `pub fn check_type(space: &DynSpace, atom: &Atom, typ: &Atom) -> bool` (L602) and `pub fn validate_atom(space: &DynSpace, atom: &Atom) -> bool` (L637). Types are atoms and checked during reduction.

**S1.Q3: Stdlib Operator Inventory**
- **Inventory:** 
    - **Arithmetic:** `+`, `-`, `*`, `/`, `%`, `<`, `>`, `<=`, `>=`, `and`, `or`, `not`, `xor`.
    - **Core:** `if-equal`, `nop`, `match`, `sealed`, `==`, `superpose`, `capture`, `pragma!`.
    - **Math:** `pow-math`, `sqrt-math`, `abs-math`, `log-math`, etc.
    - **Module:** `import!`, `include`, `bind!`, `mod-space!`.
    - **Space:** `new-space`, `add-atom`, `remove-atom`, `change-state!`, `get-state`.

**S1.Q4: Builtin_mods Coverage**
