### Gemini Report: Source 1 (OpenPsi Classic Baseline)

**1. Architecture Inventory (A1-A6)**
**Status:** Hybrid Scheme/C++ with logic split by role.
- **Scheme Core:** `action-selector.scm` (selection), `main.scm` (loop orchestration), `dynamics/` (modulators, emotions).
- **C++ Execution:** `OpenPsiImplicator.cc` (instantiation), `OpenPsiRules.cc` (indexing and PatternLink construction), `OpenPsiSatisfier.cc` (satisfiability check).
- **Rule Engine:** Rules are stored as ImplicationLink with an AndLink context, triggered via PatternLink (mapped in `OpenPsiRules::add_rule`).

**2. ECAN/STI Coupling (B1-B4)**
**Status:** Strong Runtime Coupling (2017-2018 peak).
- **Verified Formula:** `action-selector.scm:63` explicitly weights rules by `(* (cog-confidence RULE) (cog-mean RULE) (cog-av-sti RULE))`.
- **Commit `f091e9d0` (2018):** Verified the addition of `(resolve-module '(opencog attention-bank) #:ensure #f)` across `ghost.scm` and `action-selector.scm`, marking the start of making the AttentionBank optional for development flexibility.
- **GHOST Integration:** `ghost/stimulation.scm` actively uses `cog-stimulate` on word perception hooks.

**3. PLN/URE Coupling (C1-C3)**
**Status:** Minimal/Indirect.
- No direct usage of `PLN::Query` or core URE chainers was found in the OpenPsi C++ layer.
- Coupling existed primarily through the shared boolean parameter `URE:attention-allocation`, which remained an inactive placeholder (0) in most configurations.

**4. Volatility & Technical Debt (E1-E3)**
- **Obsolete File:** Commit `9d28bac0` identified `(opencog atom-types)` as the obsolete file, with logic moved to `(opencog nlp)` and `(opencog attention)`.
- **Satisfiability Cache:** `OpenPsiImplicator::check_satisfiability` (line 33) performs a cache-erase-on-every-call (`_satisfiability_cache.erase(query_body)`), confirming high-volatility/thrashing in the satisfiability path.
- **MindCloud Copyrights:** C++ files (Implicator, Rules, SCM) carry 2017 MindCloud copyrights, indicating a significant contribution period from that entity.

**5. Tradition Placement**
**Lineage:** Tradition 2 (Classic OpenCog). OpenPsi served as a mid-tier motivational and action-selection layer that bridged the high-level GHOST dialogue with the low-level ECAN currency economy, before being modularized out in 2019.
