Executive verdict
-----------------

S5 does not collapse into one tradition. It closes the remaining HAA backlog as four adjacent but distinct lines:

1. NACE is [IMPLEMENTATION-BACKED-CORE] and [AIRIS-DERIVED-NAL-ADJACENT]. It builds on AIRIS, adds partial observability and NAL-style frequency/confidence evidence, and has an explicit bridge to ONA / MeTTa-NARS. It is not AIRIS and is not itself MeTTa-NARS.
2. AI-DSL is [AI-DSL-DUAL-CITIZEN]. Its HAA-relevant slice is type-driven service/program composition in MeTTa and Idris-style dependent types, but its center of gravity is SingularityNET marketplace tooling rather than a cognitive algorithm comparable to PLN, ECAN, MOSES, or NARS.
3. MOSES is [MULTI-ERA-MOSES-LINEAGE] with active primary Hyperon-era work in iCog-Labs-Dev/metta-moses, active maintained C++ AtomSpace work in opencog/asmoses, archival pre-AtomSpace C++ in opencog/moses, and older/superseded Hyperon documentation/prototype lines in hyperon-moses and hyperon-moses2.
4. MORK-MOSES is [MORK-MOSES-CROSS-CLUSTER-OPEN-RESEARCH-LINE] with a setup-level mork_ffi hook in metta-moses but no committed mork_ffi subtree and no evidence of a working PathMap/MORK-backed MOSES implementation.
5. MeTTa-NARS is [IMPLEMENTATION-BACKED-NAL1-5] and [METTA-NARS-NOT-PLN]. It implements ONA-style NARS control and NAL 1-5 rules in MeTTa, including f/c truth functions. It should be cross-linked to PLN only as a paradigm boundary.
