§0. SOURCE IDENTITY
-------------------

This is the FINAL source of the Non-clustered Hyperon AI Algorithms
cluster pilot. It is a multi-topic source spanning four research lines
that share two common properties: each is one of the HAA Published
parents in the wiki that has not yet been audited, and each represents
a paradigm-distinct algorithmic family that is loosely coupled (or
explicitly NOT coupled) to the other HAA components.

  S5.A — NACE. patham9's "Non-Axiomatic Categorization Engine" — a
         non-axiomatic-reasoning-style categorization/learning engine,
         developer-led project with a Qt GUI. Closed-source-of-origin
         in the patham9 personal lineage (NOT an opencog/trueagi-io
         project). Cross-references with NARS but is its own engine.

  S5.B — AI-DSL. singnet/ai-dsl — domain-specific language for
         describing AI services on the SingularityNET marketplace.
         Idris-2 typed-functional implementation. Distinct from
         AGI-cognitive-architecture work; it is service-orchestration
         tooling. May or may not be in genuine HAA scope (open
         question: should this stay in HAA cluster or move to a future
         "marketplace tooling" sub-cluster?).

  S5.C — MOSES family (program-learning paradigm). The OpenCog/Hyperon
         MOSES (Meta-Optimizing Semantic Evolutionary Search) lineage:
         opencog/moses (archival pre-AtomSpace C++ MOSES);
         opencog/asmoses (AtomSpace-MOSES, the active C++ port);
         linas/as-moses (Linas Vepstas's mirror of asmoses);
         trueagi-io/hyperon-moses (early Hyperon-era port, exploratory);
         ngeiswei/hyperon-moses (Nil Geisweiller's experimental fork);
         iCog-Labs-Dev/metta-moses (the active MeTTa MOSES port — most
         active in 2026-03);
         iCog-Labs-Dev/mosesDocs (docs); plus stale forks
         (leungmanhin/asmoses, singnet/asmoses, singnet/moses,
         iCog-Labs-Dev/moses-optimization-algorithms). Plus the
         Goertzel 2025-07-21 mattermost paper "MORK-MOSES Implementation
         Ideas" which proposes integrating MOSES with the closed
         AtomSpace cluster's MORK substrate.

  S5.D — MeTTa-NARS. patham9/metta-nars — patham9's MeTTa port of
         Non-Axiomatic Reasoning System (NARS). NARS is Pei Wang's
         non-axiomatic reasoning framework, paradigm-distinct from
         PLN (closed PLN cluster pilot). MeTTa-NARS is the Hyperon-era
         port. Same author as S5.A NACE.

These four sub-sources are bundled into a single brief because:
  - All are HAA Published parents from the 2026-05-06 audit re-run
    (last 4 of the 8 HAA Published parents — AIRIS+MetaMo+Concept

    side-by-side in the wiki "Hyperon AI Algorithms" parent.
  - Reviewer extraction efficiency: a single brief reduces orchestrator
    + reviewer overhead vs four separate dispatches for what is
    largely cross-paradigm boundary verification work.
